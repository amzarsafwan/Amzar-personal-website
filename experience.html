My Journey in Sports & eSports

eSports – PUBG Mobile National Championship (PMNC)

Team: TEAM SP

Achievement: Top 2 in MEL Kebangsaan

Year: 2024

I was part of TEAM SP, competing in the PUBG Mobile National Championship (PMNC). We successfully reached the finals and secured 2nd place in MEL Kebangsaan, a national-level competition. The journey was intense and competitive, pushing me to develop strong teamwork, communication, and tactical gaming skills.

Sports – Rugby Player for Kedah U18

Team: Kedah U18 Rugby Team

Year: 2019

I represented Kedah in the Under-18 state rugby team in 2019. Competing at this level was a proud moment in my life. Rugby taught me the value of discipline, physical strength, resilience, and leadership on the field. Being part of a state team also helped me build strong bonds with fellow athletes and coaches.
